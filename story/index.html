My Story

I'm Lindsay.
I bake to bring people together.

"I have a habit of baking two loaves: one for my family, one to give away to a friend or neighbor."

My journey into sourdough was not a fairy tale. It was rocky, frustrating, and completely worth it. I fell in love with the tradition of bread making after years of struggling with killing starters, flat bread, and unintended results.

Making my own wild starter from scratch was the missing link that connected me to baking amazing bread, and then I was making my own slices of heaven.

Now, I bake nourishing sourdough from our home in Santa Rita Ranch North in Liberty Hill, for all my friends, neighbors, and community.

As the bakery grew, I found myself drawn to the traditions of pilgrimage, hospitality, and daily bread that inspired the name Fatima.

Bread rooted in tradition, hospitality, and the journey home.

What began as a daily rhythm of prayer and baking slowly became a bakery.

Naturally fermented for flavor and made with carefully sourced organic ingredients.
